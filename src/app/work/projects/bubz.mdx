## Overview

Helpnode and Bubz AI are intelligent, autonomous customer engagement platforms designed to assist businesses in providing efficient and personalized customer support. Both platforms leverage advanced AI for natural language processing, enabling real-time decision-making and handling complex customer queries.

Helpnode is tailored for enterprise clients, capable of processing millions of data points and handling unlimited simultaneous conversations. Bubz AI is designed for IGaming but offers similar features, providing advanced AI capabilities for customer support, sales, and lead generation.

I contributed to both projects by building a customizable and embeddable chat widget, developing parts of the AI pipeline, and participating in project discussions focused on algorithms and methodologies. Both platforms are highly customizable, with users able to create specific scenarios for their AI agents to follow.

## Key Features

- **Customizable Widget**: Developed an embeddable widget using React, which is highly customizable for different customers. It seamlessly integrates with existing systems, enabling businesses to engage users with context-aware and personalized conversations.
- **AI-Powered Decision Making**: Leveraged **Langchain** for advanced AI decision-making, which allowed the platform to access multiple databases, cross-check information, and make decisions in real-time.
- **Advanced Reasoning and Function-Calling**: The AI agents on both platforms can handle complex tasks, learn from scenarios, and make informed decisions based on comprehensive data across various sources, including Neo4j and SQL databases.
- **Context-Aware Conversations**: Both platforms are capable of delivering context-aware conversations based on user data, ensuring more personalized and engaging interactions.
- **Real-Time Customization**: Integrated features that allow customers to easily adjust the AI’s behavior in real-time by setting up customized scenarios and instructions.
- **Enterprise and SMB Solutions**: Helpnode was developed with enterprise-level needs in mind, processing millions of data points and ensuring seamless availability 24/7, while Bubz AI offers similar features for IGaming with a more streamlined focus on support and lead generation.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**: Built the Helpnode and Bubz AI websites using Next.js to ensure fast, scalable performance.
- **React**: Developed the embeddable chat widget in React, making it highly customizable for clients across different sectors.
- **OpenAI API**: Utilized for natural language processing and AI decision-making within both platforms.
- **Langchain**: Implemented as part of the AI pipeline, enabling advanced reasoning and decision-making capabilities for complex tasks.
- **Neo4j & MySQL**: Used for managing the backend data, including user interactions and real-time decision-making processes.
- **Redis**: Employed for fast caching and state management to ensure the systems handle large volumes of simultaneous requests efficiently.

## Challenges and Learnings

One of the primary challenges was designing a system that could handle real-time, context-aware conversations while processing vast amounts of data points simultaneously. Ensuring the AI agents could not only make informed decisions but also learn from scenarios was a key aspect of the project’s complexity. Additionally, building an embeddable widget that could be easily customized by clients required balancing flexibility with ease of use.

Through this project, I learned how to optimize real-time AI systems, integrate various data sources into a seamless decision-making pipeline, and build frontend components that are both dynamic and user-friendly.

## Outcome

Both Helpnode and Bubz AI successfully delivered powerful AI-driven customer engagement tools, with Helpnode being suited for large enterprises and Bubz AI focused on IGaming industry. The embeddable widget and advanced reasoning capabilities enhanced the platforms' usability, enabling companies to deliver fast, personalized customer service.

Helpnode and Bubz AI continue to showcase the potential of AI in customer engagement and improving user satisfaction.
