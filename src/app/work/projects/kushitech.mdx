### Dungeon Jack
An experimental crash-based, gamified adventure game where players take on the role of a hero exploring a perilous dungeon. The game combines roguelike mechanics with crash-game elements, creating a unique risk-reward gameplay loop.

**Game Mechanics:**
- Role-based gameplay: Control a hero navigating through a dungeon
- Progressive doors with randomized multiplier payouts
- Risk management: Successfully defeat all monsters behind a door to claim the multiplier payout
- Crash mechanic: Death results in losing the current bet, creating tension and engagement
- Real-time multiplier calculations and fair payout systems

**Key Features:**
- Dynamic dungeon generation with varied enemy encounters
- Real-time multiplier determination for each door
- Fair and transparent payout mechanisms
- Engaging hero progression and challenge scaling

**Live Demo:** [dungeon-jack.vercel.app](https://dungeon-jack.vercel.app)
